Price Levels Calculator

How it works:

Enter your entry price and risk percentage to calculate different price targets:

  • Lower Target (1:1): 2 levels with 50% steps
  • Middle Target (1:2): 3 levels with 33% steps
  • Upper Target (1:3): 5 levels with 20% steps

Stop-loss moves up as price reaches each level.
